Steelhead Creek Vineyards,

a special place...

A peaceful and serene place; full of wildlife and natural beauty in Sonoma County wine country. Explore 258 acres of hillside, meadows, old growth woods, ancient volcanic rocks and boulders, springs and waterfalls, miles of horse and hiking trails. Touch the clouds at 1400 feet above it all. Get lost among the native woods, commune with nature, and find your soul ...

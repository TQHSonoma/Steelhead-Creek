Just for fun

BUILDING MEMORIES.

The barn cupola has a whimsical weathervane to remind us that life is too short for "bad" wine. We are in the wine country after all; and we do celebrate often. Who knows what tomorrow will bring?  Cheers!

Bird houses on end posts

The Western blue birds love this area, especially the vineyard! They make their permanent home here. Other birds are abound, including a large population red tailed hawks, cooper hawks, horned owls, ravens, wrens, finches, robins, wood peckers, native califonia quails, turkeys. Relax and settle in for a private viewing, may it be the acrobat fight between a hawk and the crows, or a hummingbirds inches away, or the mysterious owl hoots in the evening !

Blue birds

The female blue birds are more subdued in color while the males carry a spectacular blue! They watch intently while perching on the vines and feed on insects in the vineyard rows

Organic eggs for breakfast!

Chickens enjoy the spectacular weather in Sonoma County with year-round eggs. Their "announcements" (special clucks when they lay an egg) are especially fun to hear!

A haven for favorite pets

There are 5 cats (who keep the field mice away), 2 dogs (who keep the coyotes away), 2 horses (who help keep the vineyard organic), 13 hens and a rooster (who provide plenty of great eggs) on the property. They are pretty sure this place is right next to heaven!

Garden blueberries are best!

Multiple fruit trees and plants, like blueberries, make it easy to enjoy healthful, organic treats just outside the door!

Early friendship

Animals are uncomplicated and simple. Relationships and bonds are pure magic. Reach out and touch a loved one!
